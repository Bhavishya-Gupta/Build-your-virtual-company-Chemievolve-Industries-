# CHEMIEVOLVE INDUSTRIES 
## Chemical Process Industries Project

### 📋 **Project Overview**
ChemiEvolve Industries presents a comprehensive chemical engineering project encompassing the design, synthesis, and market analysis of three key industrial chemicals: **2'-13C-L-Histidine**, **Diphenic Acid**, and **Phenol-Formaldehyde Resin (Bakelite)**. This project demonstrates complete end-to-end chemical process development from laboratory-scale synthesis to industrial implementation, including economic feasibility and environmental impact assessments.

### 🧪 **Chemical Products & Synthesis Routes**

#### **1. 2'-13C-L-Histidine**
- **Formula:** C₆H₉N₃O₂
- **Inventors:** Ansh Sethi, Raunak Jalan
- **Synthesis Route:** Multi-step process involving:
  - Synthesis of Benzyl Thiourea from potassium thiocyanate and benzyl chloride
  - Cyclization to form imidazole ring structure
  - Methylation using methyl iodide
  - Conversion to aromatic nitrile via DEPC treatment
  - Final deprotection using trifluoroacetic acid and hydrogenolysis
- **Key Features:** High chemical and optical purity with preserved imidazole ring structure

#### **2. Diphenic Acid**
- **Formula:** (C₆H₄CO₂H)₂
- **Inventors:** Nitin Gautam, Raunak Jalan
- **Synthesis Methods:**
  
  **Method 1 - Diazotization Route:**
  - Starting material: Anthranilic acid (50g)
  - Diazotization with sodium nitrite at <5°C
  - Copper(I) reduction using cupric sulfate and hydroxylamine
  - **Yield:** 88-91% (crude), 72-84% (purified)
  - **Melting Point:** 225-228°C (purified)
  
  **Method 2 - Oxidation Route:**
  - Phenanthrene oxidation with 30% H₂O₂ in glacial acetic acid
  - **Yield:** 60-67% with 96-98% purity
  - **Melting Point:** 232-233°C

#### **3. Phenol-Formaldehyde Resin (Bakelite)**
- **Formula:** (C₆H₆O·CH₂O)ₙ
- **Inventor:** Suyash Jindal
- **Synthesis Process:**
  - Condensation polymerization of phenol and formaldehyde
  - Ammonia catalyst for higher molecular weight
  - Three-stage process: Bakelite A → B → C
  - **Operating Temperature:** 160-250°F depending on reinforcement
- **Applications:** Heat-resistant materials, electrical insulators, brake components

### 🏭 **Industrial Process Designs**

#### **Formaldehyde Production**
- **Temperature Range:** 250-400°C
- **Process:** Catalytic oxidation of methanol using vanadium pentoxide
- **Equipment:** Reactors (578L, 1347L), distillation columns, heat exchangers
- **Daily Production:** 386.88 kg/day formaldehyde

#### **Bakelite Formation**
- **Conditions:** 60-100°C under reduced pressure
- **Equipment:** 1300L CSTR reactor
- **Daily Production:** 1000 kg/day phenol-formaldehyde resin
- **Capital Cost:** $47,500 (reactor systems only)

#### **Diphenic Acid Production**
- **Reactor Capacity:** 950L (anthranilic acid), 1400L (copper sulfate)
- **Process Conditions:** Ice-point cooling, controlled temperature stages
- **Capital Cost:** $50,300 (reactor systems only)

### 🌍 **Environmental Health & Safety (EHS)**

#### **Health Hazards & Controls**
| Chemical | Primary Hazards | Safety Measures |
|----------|----------------|-----------------|
| Formaldehyde | Carcinogen, respiratory irritant | Fume hood, PPE, proper ventilation |
| Phenol | Corrosive, skin absorption | Chemical-resistant gloves, eye wash stations |
| HCl (conc.) | Severe burns, toxic vapors | Splash shields, emergency shower |
| Diazonium salts | Shock-sensitive, explosive | Ice bath cooling, careful handling |

#### **Environmental Impact**
- **Air Pollutants:** Formaldehyde emissions require scrubbing
- **Aquatic Toxicity:** Copper compounds and phenol need specialized disposal
- **Waste Treatment:** Neutralization before disposal, hazardous waste facilities

#### **Disposal Methods**
- **Formaldehyde:** Permitted hazardous waste facility treatment
- **Phenol:** Incineration in approved facilities
- **Metal Compounds:** Heavy metal disposal regulations compliance
